2. Data we collect and how we use it

2.1 Data you enter in the App

The App allows you to enter and manage nutrition‑related information, including for example:

  • Foods, meals and nutrient values

This data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the App (nutrition and tracking features) and to improve your user experience.

2.2 Device permissions: Camera

The App may request access to:

Camera (android.permission.CAMERA): Used to scan barcodes and/or capture images in order to make it easier to add foods or related items. The camera feed is used only for this purpose. Images are not uploaded to our servers because we do not operate any server for this App.

You can deny this permission; in that case, the corresponding features (e.g. barcode scanning) may not work, but you can continue to use other parts of the App.

The App is designed to work offline.

If your device uses its own backups (e.g. Google backup), your data may be included in such system‑level backups according to your device/account settings. [web:149][web:155]
